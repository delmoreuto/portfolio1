About

Hello! My name is Delmore, I am a graphic designer based in Honolulu, HI. I am passionate about creating good design with a purpose and a story and am constantly looking for inspiration to grow as a designer. I enjoy creating and working on brand identities, logos, packaging, and lettering. My design experience range from all of the former, as well as print/digital ad design to editorial design. Thank you for visiting my portfolio!
